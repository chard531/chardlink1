Yos Sudarso High School

About Yos Sudarso

I attended Yos Sudarso for high school, a national school where the local language is used instead of English.The school is fairly large, with a diverse student body that includes students from all income levels.

Strengths and Challenges

Although the classrooms lack air conditioning, Yos Sudarso is known for its strong education system and has become one of the most respected schools in my hometown.
